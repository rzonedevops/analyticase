Validation & Compliance

Data validation rules and compliance requirements ensure proper integration with ZA judiciary systems

Case Number Validation

Format: (COURT_CODE) NUMBER/YEAR

Valid Examples:

(GP) 12345/2025

(WCC) 67890/2025

(GJ) 54321/2025

Invalid Examples:

GP 12345/2025

(GP) 12345-2025

(XYZ) 12345/2025

regex: ^\([A-Z]{2,3}\)\s*\d{4,6}\/\d{4}$
